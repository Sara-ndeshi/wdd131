Visit Swakopmund – Website Planning Document

Site Name

Visit Swakopmund – A tourism website promoting attractions, activities, dining, and accommodation in Swakopmund, Namibia.

Site Purpose

The website promotes Swakopmund as a travel destination by providing information about attractions, adventure activities, restaurants, accommodation options, and travel planning resources.

Scenarios

  • What adventure activities can I do in Swakopmund?
  • Where are the best restaurants and hotels near the beach?
